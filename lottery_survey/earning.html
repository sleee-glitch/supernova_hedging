Suppose you allocate 0.8 in Apple and 0.2 in Banana.

With 50% chance:
0.8 × Apple poor (-10) + 0.2 × Banana well (10) = -6
With 50% chance:
0.8 × Apple well (10) + 0.2 × Banana poor (-10) = 6

Your final payment is determined by applying probability to your potential outcome:
50% × (-6) + 50% × (6) = 0
